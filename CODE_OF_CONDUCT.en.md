# Code of Conduct

CUMT Alethic Insight Lab is a student-led research-engineering community. We expect members and contributors to maintain a serious, respectful, and learning-oriented collaboration environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical discussion.
- Critique ideas, designs, experiments, and code rather than people.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Be honest about uncertainty, limitations, and mistakes.
- Credit others' work.
- Protect sensitive information and private records.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of private recruitment, interview, governance, or candidate information.
- Publishing credentials, tokens, recovery codes, private datasets, or restricted project materials.
- Misrepresenting the organization as an official university department without authorization.
- Submitting work that you do not understand and cannot explain.

## Reporting

For conduct or safety concerns, contact github@aletheion.cn.
